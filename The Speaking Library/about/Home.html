Welcome to The Speaking Library

BEGINNING OF EXPERIENCE

The Speaking Library is a platform to encourage people who are unable to express themselves or feels they are under appreciated for their achievments.

THE CONCEPT

The Speaking Library is a platform where you can share content of your choice. Whether it be an experience, poem written/video, dance video, academic achievements, video you created, anything...

Our Contributors

People who shared their content at our website became our contributors. Anyone can be a contributor at The Speaking Library by filling this Google Form.

WHERE ARE WE TODAY

The Speaking Library, because of its Contributors has completed 100+ Publications within a month and still counting. The Content of our Contributors is bringing the audience that helps us reach more and more people everyday.
** In case of any Plagarised Content, the Contributor will be Blocked Permanently from posting any further Content **
** Achievments and Certificates will be shown on the website after Authenticating the details from the College/Society **

The Speaking Library is, in the true sense of the word, a library of speakers and performers. We host events where people can show their talents and have conversations they would not normally have access to. Every contributor is experienced in a different field. And by sharing their experience, contributors can help people, so that they don't have to start from The Scratch. Contributors are free to sahre any experience about their lifestyle, diagnosis, belief, disability, social status, ethnic origin, fear etc.
